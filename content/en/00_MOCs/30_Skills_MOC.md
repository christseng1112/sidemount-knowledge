# 🏊 In-Water Core Skills & Control (Skills MOC)

Mastering buoyancy, horizontal trim, and kicking techniques is the hallmark of a skilled sidemount diver.

- [[en/30_Skills/Second Stage Stowage, Switching & Retrieval|Second Stage Stowage, Switching & Retrieval]]: Long/short hose stowage, 30-bar regular regulator switches, and bungee necklace recovery.

### Safety Signaling & Deployments
- [[en/30_Skills/DSMB Deployment Techniques|DSMB Deployment Techniques]]: Deploying buoys under neutral buoyancy, spool release, exhaust-gas inflation, and safety limits.
