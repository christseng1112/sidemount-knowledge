# Harness Comparison (Hollis Katana 2, Dive Rite Nomad, Apeks WSX)

This knowledge base already contains in-depth articles on the [[en/20_Equipment/XDEEP Stealth 2.0 Configuration|XDEEP Stealth 2.0 Configuration]] and the [[en/20_Equipment/Razor Sidemount System Adjustment|Razor Sidemount System Adjustment]]. This article reviews three other mainstream options on the market and provides a decision-making framework: "choose your style first, then select the brand" [1].

---

## 🗺 Understanding the Two Main Styles

| Metric | **Mexican Style** | **Florida Style** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Origin Environment** | Warm freshwater caves, aluminum cylinders | Cold water, high-flow spring caves, steel cylinders |
| **Wing Shape** | Compact lift, low-profile, hugs the back | Large lift, concentrated along the spine |
| **Harness** | Adjustable webbing, separate backplates | Fixed backplates, Hogarthian-style |
| **Representative Gear** | Razor, Apeks WSX | Dive Rite Nomad, Halcyon Contour |
| **Cross-over Hybrid** | XDEEP Stealth, Hollis Katana 2 (Mexican-style silhouette designed by cold-water divers; works with both steel and aluminum) [1] | |

**Decision Sequence: Your Cylinders (Steel/Alu) & Exposure Suit (Wet/Dry) $\rightarrow$ Style $\rightarrow$ Brand Details** [1]. For cylinder buoyancy profiles, see [[en/20_Equipment/Steel vs Aluminum Buoyancy Management|Steel vs Aluminum Buoyancy Management]].

---

## ⚔ Key Features of the Three Systems

### Hollis Katana 2
*   **Market Position**: Direct competitor to the XDEEP Stealth Tec—sharing similar specs, price points, and features, with minor design differences [2].
*   **Strengths**: Harness can be configured into **either Y-strap or H-strap configurations**; highly adjustable shoulder straps make it easy to adjust and very **teaching- and rental-friendly** [3]. The wing profile compresses flat whether using steel or aluminum tanks.
*   **Considerations**: Weight slots are located along the spine plate. When carrying heavy ballast, the center of gravity shifts higher, requiring careful cylinder height alignment (see [[en/30_Skills/In-Water Buoyancy & Horizontal Trim|In-Water Buoyancy & Horizontal Trim]]).

### Dive Rite Nomad (XT / LTZ / Ray Generations)
*   **Market Position**: The classic Florida-style system. The **Nomad XT** is a heavy-duty cold-water workhorse for steel tanks, while the **LTZ and Ray** are lighter, low-profile versions designed for Mexican-style setups [1][3].
*   **Strengths**: Excellent reputation for durability. The Ray generation features **tool-free bungee length adjustments** and a zippered internal ballast pocket inside the wing [3]. The Transpac harness can be converted between sidemount and single/double backmount configurations, keeping muscle memory consistent [3].
*   **Considerations**: The XT version can feel bulky and over-sized for smaller divers or tropical diving with aluminum cylinders; those scenarios are better suited for the LTZ/Ray.

### Apeks WSX (WSX-25 / WSX-45)
*   **Market Position**: A major manufacturer's take on the Mexican minimalist style. The WSX-25 is designed for recreational single/double aluminum tank diving, while the WSX-45 is built for technical diving [1].
*   **Strengths**: Features a modular harness with a fabric-flap cover protecting the wing, providing a streamlined and abrasion-resistant shell. Backed by a global manufacturer warranty and dealer network.
*   **Considerations**: Being minimalist, it offers less adjustment range than the Katana 2. It is not designed to carry multiple heavy steel cylinders.

## 🧭 Buyer's Guide & Recommendations

1.  **Diving Cold Water / Steel Cylinders (e.g., local quarries/oceans)** $\rightarrow$ Dive Rite Nomad XT or Hollis Katana 2 (for drysuit considerations, see [[en/20_Equipment/Exposure Suit Selection & Trim Influence|Exposure Suit Selection & Trim Influence]]).
2.  **Tropical Travel / Aluminum Cylinders** $\rightarrow$ Apeks WSX-25, Nomad Ray, or the Razor/Stealth systems.
3.  **Instruction / Multi-User Sharing / Undecided** $\rightarrow$ The Hollis Katana 2 offers the most forgiving adjustment range.
4.  No matter which system you purchase, **cylinder rigging and bungee tensioning** dictate your horizontal Trim and efficiency in the water. Follow the adjustment process in [[en/20_Equipment/XDEEP Stealth 2.0 Configuration|XDEEP Stealth 2.0 Configuration]]. For buying pre-owned, consult the [[en/70_Resources/Used Sidemount Gear Buying Guide|Used Sidemount Gear Purchasing Guide]].
