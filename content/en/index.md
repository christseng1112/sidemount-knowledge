## ⚠️ Disclaimer

This site is a knowledge compilation — **not peer-reviewed literature, and no substitute for formal dive training**. Every procedure described here (valve drills, gas sharing, decompression, overhead-environment operations) is intended only as a refresher for divers already certified for that activity. In the water, follow your own agency's current standards and your instructor's guidance. Diving carries real risk; training and conservative decisions are the only things that keep you alive.
